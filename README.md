## "Finished" projects

### [Code-Geass-SRPG](https://github.com/henrylewis2004/Code-Geass-SRPG)

A recreation of the 2006 anime Code Geass: Lelouch of the Rebellion as a video game inspired by Fire Emblem built in Godot with GDscript.
The game is split into two sections: Story and Battle. With the Story sections narrating the story of the show through a visual-novel narritive and the Battle sections attempting to recreate battles from the show.

### [rubiks-solver](https://github.com/henrylewis2004/rubiks-solver)

My final year Computer Science dissertation at the University of Liverpool (2025), 
**rubiks-solver** is an application written for Android in Kotlin using Android Studio that gives voiced commands to solve a Rubiks Cube after analysing the 6 images showing each side of the cube.
